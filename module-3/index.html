Chicken

This one has spicy chicken curried in tomatoes and a variety of spices, cooked till soft and finished with cream and coriander leaves.The secret of this spicy curry are the spices used. The spice mix is made from coriander, cumin, black pepper, mustard seeds and red chili, roasted slightly over low heat before grounding.
